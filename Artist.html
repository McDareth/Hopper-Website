About the Artist


Brianna Thomas, though she most often prefers to go by her online alias/character name, Orlando Fox!

Currently resides in New York, she is a freelance cartoon artist, mostly often drawing animals/any critter or any variety! She dreams of gaining knowledge and expertise in storyboarding and animation one day, but for now, commissions along with her own for-fun art is her usual day-to-day!

Her other casual interests and hobbies include cooking/baking, tasty food in general, and video games!

You can see more of her amazing artwork on her website: afoxdraws.com
